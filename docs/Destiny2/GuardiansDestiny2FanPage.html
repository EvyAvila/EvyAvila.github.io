There are three classes players can choose from: Titan, Warlock, and Hunter. They each contain a grenade, melee, and class ability.

The Titan is considered a tank class. They
mainly play in close combat. If they used
their melee ability, their default melee is a
punch. Their class ability is putting up a
barricade to take shelter from incoming
damage. They have two types:
Towering Barricade - a large barrier for full
protection
Rally Barricade - a small barrier that allows
the player with some protection while
shooting

The Warlock is considered a mage class. They
mainly play in mid range combat. If they used
their melee ability, their default melee is a
slap. Their class ability is putting a
rift. There are two types they
can place:
Healing Rift - a small area that heals the
player(s)
Empowering Rift - a small area that
increase the weapon damage dealt from the
player(s)

The Hunter is considered an agile class. They
mainly play in full range combat. If they used
their melee ability, their default melee is a
stab with a knife. Their class ability is
dodging. There are two main types they
can use:
Markman's Dodge - the ability to reload the
weapon
Gambler's Dodge - the ability to reload the
melee class ability
