Destiny 2: Where journeys come together

Destiny 2 is a first person shooter game available for all platforms from console to steam. Taking place centuries into the future, play as a guardian from one of the three classes; Hunter, Warlock, and Titan as they fight to protect mankind from dangerous enemies. Team up with allies and your companion called Ghost.

As someone who has been playing since 2018, the game has a lot of content and activities to do. Since it's an online game played by millions around the world, players can either fight against one another or work together against enemies. Find groups through LFGs, join a clan, or load in matchmaking activities.

The activities include: Raids (6 player group), Dungeons (3 player group), Grandmasters (3 player group),
Nightfalls (3 player group), Strikes (1-3 player group), Gambit (1-4 player group), Crucible (1-6 player group)
and Trials (1-3 player group). Additionally, players can complete bounties to increase their XP and/or
earn special rewards.
