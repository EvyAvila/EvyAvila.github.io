Clan: A community where guardians unite

A clan is a community of players who play
together to complete any activities such as
raids, dungeons and more. Players can join
or be invited into the clan, where each group
can have up to a max of 100 players.

Being part of a clan also provides special
perks to the player such as rewards for gear
and weapons. Players may find or be invited
to a clan by any social or open world places,
such as the Tower, planets, or through LFG posts.

To create a clan, the player creates the name,
customize the clan banner look, and have at least
one other player join the clan. The person who
created the clan is known as the leader. The leader
has the ability to assign admin roles and set the
entrance policy.
